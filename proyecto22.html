22. Tessellation - M. C. Escher

Description

In this project I have made a drawing with a tessellation patron, as in some drawings of M. C. Escher.

Process

1. Firstly, I made the frame in two different watercolor paper sheets.

2. With another sheet, I made squares of 4cm side.

3. I cuted it with an organic shape and glued it in the shame paper, but in other position.

4. I draw a shape like a heart in one of the paper squares and in the other a shape like a very organic wall clock.

5. I tested which I liked the most and I chose the heart.

5. In the other paper which was of 20x24cm, I drew the model of the heart, moving to make the tasselation.

5. Then I coloured and the final result was the heart, and in a bigger scale a very inteserting pattern.

What have I learned?

In this project I have learned how to make a tesselle with the shape I want.
