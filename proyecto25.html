Despertando en la ciudad

Description

This is a drawing in watercolor of a street which I like in Cuenca. I had presented it to the XXVI Plastic Arts Contest.

Process

1. Firstly, I made a sketch of what I wanted to draw.

2. Then I gave it the final shape.

3. At this point, I started to paint all, starting with the sky.

4. Then I painted all with a soft base of it's corresponding colour.

5. I painted all as in the final version, then I made the final details, without the shadows.

6. I highlighted all the structures and things.

7. Then I made the shadows

What have I learned?

In this drawing I have enhanced my abilities using watercolour, drawing with proportion and using lights and shadows.
