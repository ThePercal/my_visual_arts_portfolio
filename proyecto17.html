17. Star Polygons With Coloured Wool

Description

Making a star polygon and its star polygpons with whool in a hoop.

Process

1. Firstly, I calculated how many meters of each whool I would need, and chose the colours of it.

2. Then I make the cuts in the hoop at the necessary distance.

3. I made the polygon and the stars.

4. Then I made a presentation of it explaining the saturation of the colours of the whool, the meters of whool I had used, the process...

What have I learned?

In this activity I have learned how to make a polygon of 22 sides and its star polygons in a hoop with whool.
