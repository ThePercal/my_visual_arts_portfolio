1. Tangent II. Draw Two Exterior Straight Lines Tangent to Two Circumferences

Description

Drawing two exterior straight lines tangent to two circumferences.

Process

1. Firstly, I made the frame.

2. Then I made a circumference with radius 40mm and other with radius 15mm, with a distance from O to O2 of 90mm.

3. I made a segment bisector of the distance, to get point M.

4. Then I substacted R2 (15mm), to R1, making another circumference inside it, and being the big circumference at the horizontal line in the extreme point C.

5. I made a two tangent lines with both exterior circumferences, getting T1, t1, T2 and t2.

5. I joined T1 and T2 with O.

What have I learned?

In this activity I have learned how to draw two exterior straight lines tangent to two circumferences.
