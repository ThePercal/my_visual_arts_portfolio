3. Equilateral triangle, hexagon, star hexagoneand cube

Description

In this activity we have had to draw a circumscribed regular triangle, an hexagone, a star hexagone, and a cube.

Process

1. Firstly, you need to make a frame, as we learned in the first project.

2. Then you need to set the center with an O.

3. In the point O make a circle with radius 50mm

4. Make a vertical line that passes through the centre of the circle. The top part will be point A and the bottom will be point B.

5. With the same measure, put the compass in point A and make a mark the point it touches the circumference in both directions. Repeat with point B

6. Join that points making a pentagon. name the points as C, D, E and F. E and F are connected with A, and C and D are conected with B.

7. Join O with E and F, then you will have the cube.
