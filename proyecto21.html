21. Symmetry-Textures-Colour-Elements of Visual Expression

Description

In this project we have had to make the half of an animal using the simmetry and giving it colour and texture with some shapes, in the other half to paint the animal with pencil, giving it texture

Process

1. I made the half of the drawing using symmetry.

2. Then I painted with pencil the half that was previously made, giving it texture, depth and a shape of my animal (a lion).

3. In the other half I made different types of lines in different directions and symbols and I coloured it using a chromatic order.

What have I learned?

In this activity I have learned how to make a drawing or part of it symmetric to other, to colour a drawing and giving it shape with some shapes or elements, and to paint and give texture with a pencil.
