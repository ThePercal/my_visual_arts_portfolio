20. Video Cinematic and Audiovisual Lenguage-Storybord-Shots

Description

This was the first project of the course. This activity was made for introducing us to make a frame in all the activities.

Process

1. I joined the photos of the previous exercise with CapCut.

2. Then I downloaded some sounds and put them in the video.

3. Then I put some transitions between the photos.

What have I learned?

In this activity I have learned how to make one of the most important and basic things in visual arts, the frame. I learned that without the frame, drawings can't be well organised and don't look good or have a good composition. I have also learned how to make circles with a compass, and how to use it to make circles with a certain distance between them.
