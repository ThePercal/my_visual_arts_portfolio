5. Regular Heptagon General Method When the Radius is Known

Description

In this activity we have made a regular heptagon with the general method when the radius is known

Process

1. I made the frame.

2. I made a circle of radius 50mm and set the center witn point O.

3. I divided in half vertical and horizontally. Then I made a line from point A, and divided it in 7 segments. I joined the 7 to point B and then I joined all at the shame distance.

4. I joined the point B with the point 2 of the verical line, geting the side of the heptagon.

5. I made the heptagon using the side.

What have I learned?

I have learned how to make a regular heptagon with general method when the radius is known.
