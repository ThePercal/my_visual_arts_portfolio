9. Regular Pentagon Particular Method when the Side is Known and Star Pentagon

Description

Making a regular pentagon with the particular method when the side is known and the star pentagon that can be obtained from it.

Process

1. Firstly, I made the frame.

2. Then I made a segment (side) of 50mm and I elongated it.

3. Then I made a segment bisector between A and B and elongating it, getting point M.

4. Then I got point S, and used it to get point T.

5. Then I made the pentagon.

6. Finally, I got the star pentagon.

What have I learned?

I have learned how to make a regular pentagon with the particular method when the side is known and the star pentagon that can be obtained from it.
