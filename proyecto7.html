7. Regular Pentagon Particular Method When the Radius is Known and Star Pentagon

Description

Making a regular pentagon with the particular method when the radius is known and the star pentagon that can be made with it.

Process

1. I made the frame.

2. Then I made a circle of 50mm radius and divided it vertical and horizontally.

3. I made an arch fron the extreme of horizontal line in the right, to the center.

4. I joined the two points that were formed, and the point that passed through the horizontal line was called M.

5. I made an arch from M to A, the resultant point was called B. Then I joint A and the other extreme and called it S.

6. With point S, from point A, I made the pentagon.

6. Then I joined the points skipping one, to get the star pentagon.

What have I learned?

In this activity I have learned how to make a regular pentagon with the particular method when the radius is known and the star pentagon that can be made with it.
