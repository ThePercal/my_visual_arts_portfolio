8. Regular Nonagon Particular Method When the Radius is Known and Star Pentagon

Description

Making a regular nonagon particular method when the radius is known and the star pentagon that can be obtained from it.

Process

1. I made the frame.

2. I made a circle of radius 50mm and divided it vertical and horizontally, and set the center with point O.

3. I made an arch from point A to O, and another from B to O, getting point E and F.

4. I extended the horizontal line, and made an arch from E to F and vice versa, to get point T.

5. I made an arch from T to A, getting the "side 9".

6. With that point, from A I made the nonagon.

7. Finally, I made the star nonagon joining all points, skipping one.

What have I learned?

In this activity I have learned how to make a regular nonagon particular method when the radius is known and the star pentagon that can be obtained from it.
