4. Square, octogon and star octogon when the radius is known

Description

In this activity I have made a square, an octogon and a star octogon when the radius is known.

Process

1. First, I made the frame.

2. Then I made a circle of radius 50mm

3. Then I divided it in half vertical and horizontally.

4. Then I seted the center (O) and the points A, B, C and D. Then I joined them, making the first square.

5. I made the segment bisector of them, to get 8 points, I joined them and I got the octogon

6. I joined point 2, 4, 6 and 8, to make the second square.

7. I joined all points skiping two, then I get the star octogon.

What have I learned?

In this activity I have learned how to make a square, an octogon and an star octogon, circumscribed in a circle with only knowing the radius.
