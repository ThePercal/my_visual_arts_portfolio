18. Light and Shadows

Description

This has been an activity we have made in class during some days, to practise making texture with lights and shadows.

Process

1. I coloured first the bars, making them a degraded, having only two minites.

2. The spheres have been coloured during four minutes, giving them depth with a pencil.

What have I learned?

In this activity I have learned how to give texture to an object with the lights and shadows.
