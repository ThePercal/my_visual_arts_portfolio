11. Star Polygon +13

Description

In this activity I made an star polygon of 22 sides and the possible stars to create with it.

Process

1. Firstly, I made the frame.

2. Then I made a circle as big as possible, and using the precess of Activity 4, I made a polygon of 22 sides.

3. Then I made as much star polygons as I could with it, making a total of ten.

4. I coloured all the stars.

What have I learned?

In this activity I have learned how to make a polygon of the number of sides I wanted and making a lot of star polygons with it.
