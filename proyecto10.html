10. Regular Polygons From 6 to 12 General Method When the Side is Known

Description

In this activity we needed to make a combination of regular polygons from 6 to 12 with a general method when the side is knwon.

Process

1. Firstly, you need to make a frame at a distance of one centimeter from the edges of the paper. It consist of two horizontal and two vertical parallel lines. Remember to leave a gap between the sides and the edges of the paper.

2. Then, you need to make a parallel line to the horizontal line at the bottom of the paper, at a distance of 3cm. This line will be the base of the data of the project.

3. The divide the information space in three parts, one of 3cm long, parallel to the left vertical side of the paper, and two equal to this in the other side of the paper. Then make a line of 1cm, parallel to the top hozintal line of the box, and another of 1cm to the last we have made, but only in right side of the paper, in the 6cm of the two boxes.

4. Then, find out the center of the box, mark it with a a cross and a little circle.

5. Finally, make a circle with a radius of 0.5cm, which makes a circle of 1cm diameter. From that circle make circles from a distance of 1cm, until you can't do more circles in the "box".

What have I learned?

In this activity I have learned how to make a combination of regular polygons from 6 to 12 with a general method when the side is knwon.
