10. Regular Polygons From 6 to 12 General Method When the Side is Known

Description

In this activity we needed to make a combination of regular polygons from 6 to 12 with a general method when the side is knwon.

Process

1. I made the frame.

2. I made a segment (side), of 40mm, and got a triangle from it.

3. Using the same process as in Activity 4, I made all the polygons from 6 to 12, and 3 to five with the methods of previous activities.

4. Then I coloured it in a chromatic order.

What have I learned?

In this activity I have learned how to make a combination of regular polygons from 6 to 12 with a general method when the side is knwon.
