2. My Bedroom

Description

This was the second activity of the course. This activity was made for introducing the concept of the perspective, the colour, and the lights and shadows in the drawing. We had to draw our bedroom in a perspective way.

Process

1. Firstly, you need to make a frame, as we learned in the first project.

2. Then, you need to make an sketch of your bedroom, with all the furniture and objects you have in it. You can use a pencil for this step, because you will need to erase some lines. For being more pure, you can make the sketch with a colour pencil, but you need to be more careful.

3. When you have the sketch, you need to make the final drawing, with all the objects and furniture.

4. When you have the final drawing, you need to locate the lights and shadows. After that, start to colour softly the drawing.

5. Progressively, you need to colour more and more the drawing, until you have the final result, with a good colour and a good light and shadow.

What have I learned?

In this activity I have learned how to make a drawing with perspective, and how to use the colour, the light and the shadow in a drawing. I have also learned how to make a sketch, and how to make a final drawing from that sketch.
