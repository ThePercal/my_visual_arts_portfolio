23. Landscape in Four Seasons in tempera

Description

This has been the final activity of the course, and has consisted in making a drawing of a landscape in the four different seasons in tempera, at a high level.

Process

1. I made the frame

2. Then I divided the paper into 4 parts

3. Then I made an sketch of each of the seasons.

4. I started colouring spring, first the sky, then the mountains, the soil and the trees. Then I made the final details and finished it, making the background less saturated.

5. I repeated the process with the other three seasons, with each respective colours.

What have I learned?

In this activity I have learned how to paint correctly with watercolours, how to use the pallet, and prepare the drawing space.
